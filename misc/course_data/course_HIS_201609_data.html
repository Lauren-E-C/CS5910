Modern Japan - 92716 - HIS 385 - 800
This course investigates the culture and history of modern Japan. Chronologically, it exams four significant periods in the history of Modern Japan: 1. the Era of the Pax Tokugawa (ca. 1600 to 1800s) to the Meiji Restoration (1868), 2. The Pre-War Japan (1868-1937), 3. the Wartime Era (1937-1945), and 4. the Post-War Era (1945 to present.) Thematically, the course focuses on Tradition and Modernity. How Japan has been dealing with its own tradition in the course of modernization?
